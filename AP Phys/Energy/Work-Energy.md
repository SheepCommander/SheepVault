$W=fd\cos \theta$ 
> Force applied must be **in the direction** of movement.

$W=\Delta K_{f}=\Delta U_{g}=\Delta U_{s}=\Delta E_{th}$ 
$Fd=\frac{1}{2}mv^2=mgh=\frac{1}{2}kx^2=f_{k}\Delta x$ 
> $E_{th}$ and $f_{k}\Delta x$ are NOT mechanical energy.
