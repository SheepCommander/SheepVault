FTC Part 1:
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}\int_{a}^{u}f(t)dt&=f(u)u' \\
F(t)_{\vert_{a}^{x}}&=F(x)-F(a) \\
\frac{d}{dx}[F(x)-F(a)]&=F'(x)-0=f(x) \\
\end{align}$$
FTC Part 2:
$$\begin{align}
\int_{a}^{b}f(t)dt=F(t)\vert_{a}^{b}=F(b)-F(a)
\end{align}$$
Important for $A(x)=\int _1^xf(x)dx$  and $A(1)=3$
$$\begin{align}
A(4)&=A(1)+\int_{1}^4f(x)dx\\
\end{align}$$
