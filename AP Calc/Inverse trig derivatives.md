A bunch of implicit differentiation, trig, and chain rule
$$\begin{align}
y&=\arcsin x \\
\sin y&= x \\
\sin \theta&=\frac{o}{h}=\frac{x}{1} \\
o^2+a^2&=h^2 \\
x^2+a^2&=1^2 \\
a&=\sqrt{ 1-x^2 } \\
\frac{d}{dx}\sin y&=\frac{d}{dx}x \\
\cos (y) * \frac{dy}{dx}&=1 \\
\frac{dy}{dx}&=\cos(y) =\frac{a}{h}=\frac{\sqrt{ 1-x^2 }}{1} \\
\frac{dy}{dx}&=\frac{\sqrt{ 1-x^2 }}{1}
\end{align}$$
